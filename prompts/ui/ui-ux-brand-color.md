##
Act as a Senior Product Designer and Senior Frontend Architect.

Design a complete UI/UX system for a modern freelance marketplace platform called "SkillSwap".

Project Overview:
SkillSwap is a freelance micro-task marketplace where Clients post tasks, Freelancers submit proposals, Clients hire freelancers, make Stripe payments, and Admins manage the platform.

Design Goals:
- Premium SaaS Design
- Modern Startup Feel
- Professional and Trustworthy
- Clean and Minimal
- Similar quality to Fiverr, Upwork, Linear, Notion, and Stripe
- Fully Responsive
- Accessible Design
